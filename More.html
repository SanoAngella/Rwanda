Rwanda not only has the 5 main projects, but also has many other crucial projects

Major Development Projects in Rwanda

Rwanda is rapidly transforming through several key projects aimed at boosting its economy, energy, and infrastructure. Some of the most important include:

  1. KivuWatt Power Plant
      A modern hub for tech, education, and business, bringing universities, research centers,
      and startups together in one place.
  2. Gisagara Thermal Power Station
      A peat-fired plant aimed at boosting Rwanda’s energy supply, especially in regions that
      need more reliable power.
  3. Gasmeth Plant (Gas Works)
      Linked with the methane project, this plant processes and uses gas for energy,
      making the system more efficient and integrated.
  4. Vision city
      Large-scale housing development intended to offer modern, affordable homes for
      Kigali’s growing population.
  5. Inzovu Mall/ Commercial Developments
      Big commercial and retail complexes in Kigali to meet rising demand for shopping,
      entertainment, and business spaces.
  6. Equity Twin Towers/ Office & Mixed-Use Skyscrapers
      Tall buildings to host offices, services, and commercial functions — elevating
      Kigali’s skyline and business infrastructure.
  7. Twin Lakes City, Hospitality Zones
      Developing tourist destinations (especially around Burera & Ruhondo) with hotels,
      nature sites, and attractions.
  8. Kigali Urban Transport Upgrades
      Road improvements, better junctions, and more organized public transport to
      minimize congestion and ease movement.
